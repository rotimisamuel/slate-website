Technology Strategy

Multi-year technology strategies aligned with your institutional goals and the operational realities of your sector — government ministry, university, bank, or enterprise.

We produce strategy documents that are actionable, not aspirational: sequenced priorities, resource requirements, risk assessments, and clear success metrics.

ICT Governance

Frameworks and policies ensuring technology investments deliver value and manage risk — covering data governance, information security, procurement controls, and technology risk management.

We help institutions establish governance structures that enable digital progress while meeting regulatory obligations and protecting organisational assets.

Vendor Management

Independent vendor evaluation, RFP design, contract review, and performance monitoring — protecting your organisation from vendor lock-in and underperformance.

We have evaluated vendor relationships across ERP, cloud, telecoms, and custom software categories for public sector and enterprise clients across Africa.

Digital Investment

Business case development and investment appraisal — ensuring every pound or naira spent on technology is justified by evidence and structured for maximum return.

We build the financial and strategic cases that get technology investment approved at board and ministerial level, including total cost of ownership analysis and benefits realisation frameworks.
