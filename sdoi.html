How It Works

Flexible ownership
for every student.

The Student Device Ownership Initiative (SDOI) is Slate's flagship partnership programme with universities and colleges across Nigeria and the UK. Students receive a premium device on day one and pay over time through a transparent, fair instalment plan.

01
University Enrols

The institution partners with Slate by signing a zero-obligation MoU, granting students access to the programme.

02
Student Registers

Eligible students register using their student ID. No credit check, no guarantor, no upfront payment required.

03
Device Delivered

Students receive their chosen device — laptop, tablet, or smartphone — on campus, fully set up by our team.

04
Pay Monthly

Repayments over 12–24 months at a transparent 3% monthly fee on the declining balance. Settle early any time.
