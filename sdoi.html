Slate Digital Ownership Initiative

Bridging
the digital divide.

A Slate initiative to put intelligent devices in the hands of students, educators, and workers across Africa — through subsidised hardware, partnership programmes, and digital literacy support.

The initiative

Devices for
those who
need them most.

The Slate Digital Ownership Initiative (SDOI) is our flagship programme to address the device-access gap that holds back education and workforce participation across the continent.

Through partnerships with NGOs, ministries of education, and corporate sponsors, we deliver subsidised laptops, tablets, and connectivity packages — bundled with digital literacy training and ongoing technical support.

How SDOI works

Three pillars.
One outcome.

Subsidised Devices

Purpose-built Slate laptops and tablets at significantly reduced cost, funded through a blend of corporate sponsorship, donor partnerships, and Slate's own commitment.

Connectivity Packages

Bundled data plans negotiated with telco partners to ensure devices are not just delivered but actually usable for education and work.

Digital Literacy

Embedded training programmes — both within our LMS platforms and in-person — that ensure recipients gain real productive capability, not just hardware.

Lifecycle Support

Multi-year warranty, repair, and replacement support so devices remain productive throughout their useful life — not abandoned at the first fault.
