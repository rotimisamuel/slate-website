Who We Are

Technology that serves real lives

Slate Technology Ltd is a homegrown technology company with a singular conviction: access to quality devices should never be a privilege. Founded with offices in Abuja, Nigeria and London, United Kingdom, we design, manufacture, and distribute a comprehensive range of high-performance electronic devices built for the realities of African life — and the ambitions of a global generation.

Our philosophy is disarmingly simple: Technology should be accessible, reliable, and empowering. Every product we engineer reflects this belief — from the dustproof construction of our field-ready devices to the elegance of our UPS systems that complement a premium interior rather than dominate it.

From classrooms in Kano to boardrooms in Lagos, from field engineers in Nairobi to entrepreneurs in London — Slate devices are crafted to make everyday life smarter, more efficient, and more connected. We pair cutting-edge innovation with practical affordability, backed by flexible ownership models that put the best technology within reach of everyone.

We are not merely a distributor. We are architects of a smarter, more connected Africa — building local capability while meeting global standards, one device at a time.

Purpose & Direction

Guided by a clear north star

Mission

To empower individuals and organisations with innovative and dependable technology solutions that enhance productivity, creativity, and connectivity — while ensuring affordability and accessibility through flexible ownership models that remove financial barriers to participation in the digital economy.

M
Vision

To become Africa's leading and most trusted homegrown technology brand — recognised globally for creating intelligent, durable, and elegantly designed products that meet international standards while honouring local realities, aspirations, and the environments in which our users thrive.

V
Strategic Objectives
