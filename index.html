Slate Technologies Limited is a software development, technology solutions, and digital transformation company — with deep capabilities in AI strategy, e-learning implementation, and technology consulting. With offices in Abuja and London, we operate across two continents.

We build bespoke software platforms, design and deploy large-scale digital transformation programmes, implement AI-powered systems for enterprises and governments, and manufacture high-quality hardware engineered for African realities.
